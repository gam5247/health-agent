# Medical Disclaimer

This project is for software development, research prototyping, and competition
preparation only.

It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or
clinical eligibility determinations. Any clinical-trial match produced by this
software must be reviewed by qualified clinical professionals against the
official trial protocol and the patient's complete medical record.

Do not use this repository to process protected health information unless an
appropriate privacy, security, compliance, and consent framework is in place.
